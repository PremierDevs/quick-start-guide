The Big Picture

Leads arrive from everywhere — website forms, Zillow, Realtor.com, referrals, sign calls, open houses, live chat. However they come in, they land in DealFlow as a Lead. You qualify them quickly, create a Deal, and the lead phase ends. But the relationship doesn't.

After the deal closes, that Person stays in your database. You keep in touch — birthdays, anniversaries, quarterly check-ins. When they're ready to buy or sell again, you create a new Deal directly from their record. No need to make them a lead again.

Key Concept The lead phase is just the front door — a short discovery period where you learn what they need and whether you're the right fit. Once qualified, the Person record is where the lifelong relationship lives. One Person, many Deals over a lifetime.

Getting Help & Giving Feedback

Help is built into every page and we want your feedback.

Help System

  • Click the ? button in the top nav on any page
  • A drawer slides open with help specific to that page
  • Covers what everything does, workflows, and tips
  • Press Esc to close

Feedback & Bug Reports

  • Click the + button in the top nav bar
  • Submit bugs, suggestions, or feature requests
  • Includes automatic screenshot & context snapshot
  • Admin reviews and responds — your input shapes the system
Don't Struggle in Silence If something is confusing, broken, or could be better — tell us. The help drawer and feedback button are always one click away.

Working Your Leads

Status What It Means Your Action
Never Contacted Brand new — no outreach yet Call or text within 5 minutes
Awaiting Response You've reached out, waiting to hear back Follow up, try different channels
Ready for Appointment Qualified & ready for a meeting or showing Set the appointment, then convert to a Deal

Daily Workflow

  1. Go to Leads — click the Never Contacted filter
  2. Click into each lead — review info, source, tracking data
  3. Make contact and log the activity in the composer
  4. Work through the Pre-Qualification Checklist
  5. When qualified, click Convert to Client to create a Deal

Managing Deals

Prospecting → Active → Offer → Under Contract → Closed
Stage What's Happening
Prospecting Client is searching — setting up alerts, showing properties
Active Listing is live or buyer is actively seeing homes
Offer Offer submitted, negotiating
Under Contract Binding agreement — inspections, appraisal, closing prep
Closed Done! Commission earned.
Advancing Stages Click Advance Stage at the top of a deal. "Under Contract" opens a modal for binding/closing dates. "Active" on a listing prompts for the MLS number.
